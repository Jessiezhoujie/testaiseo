Explanation of Factors

Age of Home: The age of the mobile home affects its depreciation, with older homes generally being worth less.

Size of Home: Larger homes tend to have higher values due to more living space.

Condition of Home: Homes in excellent or good condition will have a higher value than those in fair or poor condition.

Number of Rooms: More rooms can increase the value of the home, as it provides more space for living.

Manufacturer: Certain manufacturers are known for higher-quality homes, which can influence resale value. Premium brands tend to have higher value, while budget brands are typically worth less.

Calculation Logic

The value of a mobile home is estimated based on the above factors, including the home's age, size, condition, number of rooms, and the manufacturer's reputation. The final value is calculated by combining these factors, with weight adjustments based on each variable.
